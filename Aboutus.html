Our Values


Support:Our company will be willing to help animals that are in need of our help.
Convience:Our company will not only be selling food and toys for pets but also be providing services which will be the perfect place for pet owners to help take care of their pets not only in the inside but as well as the outside.
Passion:We started our company because we love pets and care for their well-being.
Quality:Our company will only have the highest quality products.


About our company

The paw place has been around since 1980s, since the days it was first created by
meowmaster the third to help improve the lives of animals. Now the shop has
expanded to many more locations with much more to offer, to help pet owners
pick out what they want for their animals like food, beds etc. This makes things
more convenient for the daily lives of pet owners.
We created this website because we wanted to provide good service
to animals that we very much care for and think they deserve the
quality of life as we receive as humans. So we specialise in quality products
that are organic and free of chemicals. Our services include carefully
thought procedures to care for your pets.
